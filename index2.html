Travel & stay

Getting there

Getting there

Fly into Mexico City International (MEX) or Felipe Ángeles (NLU). March is beautiful and the city fills up fast, so book flights early — your future self will thank you.

Getting around is easy: Uber and taxis are plentiful and inexpensive across the city.

Where to stay

We'll share a hotel block soon. In the meantime, Roma Norte and Condesa are our picks — leafy, walkable, and close to everything, with great cafés and restaurants on every corner.

Questions in the meantime? Reach us any time — we're happy to help you plan.

Good to know

When should I RSVP by?

Please let us know by the date on your invitation so we can finalize headcount with the venue. Sooner is always better if you already know — it helps us plan.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Your invitation will note who's included. If you're unsure, just ask — we're easy.

What should I wear?

Cocktail attire. Bring shoes you can dance in — the floor will be busy, and comfortable feet last longer.

Should I make a trip of it?

Please do. CDMX is one of the best cities in the world to spend a few days — museums, markets, food you'll think about for years. Come early, stay late, and let us point you to our favorites.
