Our story

How we got here

Some love stories are a slow build and some are a lightning strike. Ours was a bit of both — a first conversation that ran far too long, and every one since that we haven't wanted to end.

A few years, a couple of cities, and one very good decision later, we're doing the thing. We'd love for you to be there when we do — twice, if you can manage it.

Travel & stay

Getting there

Mexico City

Fly into Mexico City International (MEX) or Felipe Ángeles (NLU). We'll share a hotel block soon — Roma Norte and Condesa are our picks for staying close to everything.

March is beautiful and the city fills up, so book flights early.

The Farm

Details and a map are on the way. There'll be parking on site and a few recommended places to stay nearby for anyone traveling in.

Questions in the meantime? Reach us any time — we're happy to help you plan.

Good to know

Do I need to come to both celebrations?

Not at all. Come to whichever works for you — or both, if you're up for the adventure. We'll be thrilled to see you at either.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Your invitation will note who's included. If you're unsure, just ask — we're easy.

What should I wear?

Garden formal at the farm, cocktail attire in Mexico City. Comfortable shoes are your friend at both.

Will there be lawn games?

Obviously. Grab a friend and play a game — that's an order, not a suggestion.

How do I RSVP?

Tap the RSVP button anywhere on this page — it takes about a minute. Please respond by the date on your invitation so we can finalize counts.
